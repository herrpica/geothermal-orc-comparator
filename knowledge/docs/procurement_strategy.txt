PROCUREMENT STRATEGY COST MODEL — CALIBRATION REFERENCE
========================================================

Domain: economics_market
Tags: procurement_strategy, self_perform, direct_purchase, geoblock, validated

1. STRATEGY DEFINITIONS
-----------------------

OEM Lump-Sum (oem_lump_sum):
  Equipment: OEM integrated package (Turboden, Exergy, ORMAT)
  Construction: EPC lump-sum contractor
  OEM integration margin: 30-40% on equipment
  EPC contractor markup: 25-35% on construction
  Target: ~$2,500/kW installed (surface facility)
  Validated: Turboden 93 MW at $2,566/kW actual

Direct Lump-Sum (direct_lump_sum):
  Equipment: Direct vendor purchase (turbine from OEM, HX from fabricator, ACC from Worldwide)
  Construction: EPC lump-sum contractor
  Eliminates OEM integration margin on equipment
  Retains EPC markup on construction
  Target: ~$1,800-2,200/kW installed

OEM Self-Perform (oem_self_perform):
  Equipment: OEM integrated package
  Construction: Owner T&M self-perform (Fervo's own construction team)
  Retains OEM integration margin on equipment
  Eliminates EPC contractor markup on construction
  Target: ~$1,900-2,200/kW installed

Direct Self-Perform (direct_self_perform):
  Equipment: Direct vendor purchase
  Construction: Owner T&M self-perform
  Eliminates both OEM and EPC margins
  Requires in-house procurement & construction management capability
  Target: ~$1,600-1,900/kW installed (GeoBlock thesis)


2. VALIDATED EQUIPMENT COSTS (DIRECT PURCHASE)
-----------------------------------------------

Turbine-Generator Package:
  Exergy radial outflow: ~$77/kW (validated, 100+ installations)
  Conventional axial: ~$200-250/kW (Baker Hughes, Mitsubishi)
  Model uses $200/kW for direct_lump_sum/direct_self_perform

3. SELF-PERFORM CONSTRUCTION MODEL (T&M RATES)
-----------------------------------------------

BOP Piping & Valves: 12% of equipment (vs 16% EPC)
  Savings from direct pipe procurement and owner-managed labor

Civil & Structural: $90/kW gross (vs $260/kW EPC)
  Bulk earthwork and concrete at T&M rates
  No contractor mobilization/demobilization markup

E&I Installation: $115/kW gross (vs $190/kW EPC)
  Direct hire electricians and instrument techs
  Owner provides supervision

Construction Labor: $185/kW gross (vs $285/kW EPC)
  T&M basis, no lump-sum profit margin
  Includes owner CM staff overhead

Engineering & Procurement: 7% of equipment (vs 10% EPC)
  In-house engineering team, direct vendor management

Commissioning & Startup: $48/kW gross (vs $65/kW EPC)
  Owner-led commissioning with vendor field service

Contingency: 9% (vs 10% EPC)
  Lower due to owner risk tolerance and schedule control


4. GEOBLOCK THESIS
------------------

Core premise: Geothermal developer with construction capability can
achieve $700-1,000/kW cost advantage over traditional OEM+EPC model.

Direct + Self-Perform eliminates:
  - OEM integration margin (30-40% on equipment)
  - EPC contractor profit (25-35% on construction)
  - Duplicated project management overhead
  - Markup on markup (contingency on inflated base)

Required capabilities:
  - Procurement team with equipment vendor relationships
  - Construction management staff experienced in power plant erection
  - QA/QC program meeting ASME, NEC, and local jurisdiction requirements
  - Field engineering support during construction
  - Commissioning and startup expertise

Risk factors:
  - Owner assumes construction risk (schedule, quality, safety)
  - Equipment warranty management is owner's responsibility
  - No single-point-of-contact for performance guarantee
  - May need to carry higher contingency if team is inexperienced
